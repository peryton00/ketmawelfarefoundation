About Us

Building a Better Tomorrow Through Compassion

Ketma Welfare Foundation is dedicated to creating sustainable change in underserved communities. Through our comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, and community development, we empower individuals to break the cycle of poverty and build brighter futures.

Our Impact

Creating Ripples of Change

Education Programs

Providing quality education and scholarships to underprivileged children, opening doors to brighter futures.

  • 10,000+ students supported
  • 50+ schools built
  • 500+ scholarships awarded

Healthcare Initiatives

Delivering essential medical care and health awareness to communities lacking access to healthcare facilities.

  • 100+ medical camps organized
  • 25,000+ patients treated
  • 15 mobile health units

Community Development

Building infrastructure and empowering communities with skills training and sustainable livelihood programs.

  • 30+ villages transformed
  • 2,000+ families empowered
  • 100+ wells constructed
